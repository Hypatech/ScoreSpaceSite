ABOUT
Scorespace is an innovative, new type of gamejam, that involves both gamedevs and gamers in the contest. This is done by allowing players to compete for a high score in the winning jam games. After the game developers have created the games and the judges have chosen the winners, it is up to the gamers to achieve the highest score in each game.

DEVELOPERS
The jam starts with the game developers - their job is to create a game within a 72 hour period. Once those three days are over, there is a three day judging period in which the games are judged by the jam participants. The top three games receive a prize and a YouTube video announcing their games.

GAMERS
After the video is uploaded, the torch is then passed to the gamers. The gamers have a 72 hour period (see a pattern?) to play the winning games. Whichever player achieves the highest score in each game also receives a prize, and a youtube video announcing their names.
