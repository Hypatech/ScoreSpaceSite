THE THEME FOR THE JAM IS: WAVES!
Waves are absolutely everywhere - sound waves, sea waves, shockwaves or even waves of zombies or orcs! This makes it a perfect theme; your game could be anything from a wave-based shooter to an experimental puzzle game involving sound. We're sure that the community will come up with many crazy ideas that didn't even cross our minds for a second. If you have a great idea...
Get out there and make it!

PARTICIPATION
Participation is easy. Check out our GameJolt Page and read the instructions there if you'd like to participate.

When tweeting about the jam, be sure to include #scorejam so you can get our attention!


SCHEDULE
Jam Start: 9:00pm PST, Friday January 4th
When the jam starts, developers must immediately start making. THIS IS NOW, GUYS! WE'VE ALREADY STARTED!

Dev Time End: 9:00pm PST, Monday January 7th
Developer time ends, and there is a short break. This allows the devs to rest and lets the community as a whole look at the games made in the jam.

Rating Start: 9:00pm PST, Thursday January 10th
The winners of the development portion of the jam get chosen to move onto the gaming section.

Gaming Start: 9:00pm PST, Friday January 11th
After the development winners are chosen, the gamer portion of the jam starts.

Gaming End: 9:00pm PST, Monday January 14th
The jam comes to a close as the gaming portion ends.
