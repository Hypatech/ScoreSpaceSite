GAMEDEVS

Gamedevs are tasked with competing in the first half of the jam. In this half, they are required to develop a game in a limited amount of time (72 hours.) The top three games are chosen, and then placed in a highlight video made for the specific jam.

HOW TO PARTICIPATE
1

Check the theme and rules, making sure you're following them.

2

Develop your game over the 72 hours you're given.

3

Once you think your game is done, upload it to itch.io and fill in the submission form

4

Wait to see if you've won the jam!

RULES
1

You MUST fill in the signup form after you have finished your game.

2

Your game MUST have some kind of scoring system (online or offline)

3

If your game is NSFW, there must be some kind of warning at the start and in the description.

4

You are not allowed to assist any streamers to obtain a higher score in your game. You will be disqualified if you do so.

5

You and your team must make everything from scratch during the event

6

All games must be in digital format playable on Linux, Windows, Mac or the Web.
