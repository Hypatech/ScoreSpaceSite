ABOUT

Scorespace is an innovative type of gamejam in which the wider community outside of game developers are able to get involved too. The jam consists of two halves: gamedevs and streamers. The jam begins with the gamedevs and ends with the streamers.

GAMEDEVS

Gamedevs are tasked with competing in the first half of the jam. In this half, they are required to develop a game in a limited amount of time (72 hours.) The top three games are chosen, and then placed in a highlight video made for the specific jam.

STREAMERS

Streamers are tasked with competing against each other in the second half of the jam. In this half, they are required to obtain the highest score in one of the winning three games and submit a video recording of them doing so.
