STREAMERS

Streamers are tasked with competing against each other in the second half of the jam. In this half, they are required to obtain the highest score in one of the winning three games and submit a video recording of them doing so.

HOW TO PARTICIPATE
1

Wait for the winning games to be announced.

2

Stream the game, and make sure you are able to capture some kind of recording (VOD or external software)

3

If you're happy with your final score, submit the video of yourself achieving it to our signup form.

4

Wait to see if you've won the jam!

RULES
1

You MUST fill in the signup form after you have your final score.

2

You MUST submit some kind of recording, i.e a VOD

3

Any game-altering software (even with developer consent) is NOT allowed.

4

If you're a developer, you cannot play your own game. You can, however, play games made by other contestants.
