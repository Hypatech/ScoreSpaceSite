THE THEME IS... DESTRUCTION!

Usually, we like to keep our themes pretty vague and tame. Let's change that.
We want you to make games about DESTROYING! We want to crush things and LAUGH as they crumble!

NO MORE GROWTH! JUST DESTRUCTION! MUAHAHAHAHAHAAAHAAA!

...all jokes aside, the theme for our next jam is destruction! We felt like this would be a really fun one to both play and create, and we hope you feel the same too. So go out there and make us some fun games!

If you'd like to join, please go to our itch page for instructions on how to participate! Have fun!

SCHEDULE

Jam Start: 9:00pm PST, Friday
When the jam starts, developers must immediately start making their game.

Dev Time End / Rating Start: 9:00pm PST, Monday
Developer time ends, and the rating period begins. This is where the best games get voted by the other participants and the winners are decided.

Rating End: 9:00pm PST, Thursday
The winners of the development portion of the jam get chosen to move onto the streaming section.

Gaming Start: 9:00pm PST, Friday
After the development winners are chosen, the streamer portion of the jam starts.

Gaming End: 9:00pm PST, Sunday
The jam comes to a close as the streamer portion ends.
