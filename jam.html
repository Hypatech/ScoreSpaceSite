THE THEME IS... UNDERGROUND!

There's undeniable a sense of mystery to do with everything underground, whether that means simply below the earth or outside-of-mainstream view. This is why we picked it as our theme for our latest jam; there is so much potential for creativity!

As usual, every game must have a high-score system in place (online or offline) and must follow every rule laid out in the gamedev page. For the streamer side of the jam, simply make sure you are able to record your screen and send the recording to us.

In order to submit, visit our itch.io page, and read the instructions on there!

SCHEDULE

Jam Start: 9:00pm PST, Friday March 1st
When the jam starts, developers must immediately start making their game.

Dev Time End / Rating Start: 9:00pm PST, Monday March 4th
Developer time ends, and the rating period begins. This is where the best games get voted by the other participants and the winners are decided.

Rating End: 9:00pm PST, Thursday March 7th
The winners of the development portion of the jam get chosen to move onto the streaming section.

Gaming Start: 9:00pm PST, Friday March 8th
After the development winners are chosen, the streamer portion of the jam starts.

Gaming End: 9:00pm PST, Sunday March 10th
The jam comes to a close as the streamer portion ends.
